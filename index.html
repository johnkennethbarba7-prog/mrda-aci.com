About:

MRDA Academic Center Inc. was founded in the year of 2018 together with Mrs. Elaine A. Daliva and Mr. Michael R. Dupal-Ag. It is currently located in San Isidro, Calabanga, Camarines Sur. The school is committed to offering inclusive, quality, and affordable education to all members of its community. The institution prides itself on fostering an environment where excellence is not just a goal, but a standard upheld by students and faculty alike. With deep roots in the local community, MRDA Academic Center seeks to empower individuals through accessible and comprehensive educational programs.

Driven by a forward-thinking vision, MRDA Academic Center aspires to become the Center of Development in Basic Education, Technology Education, and Research by the year 2035. This ambitious goal reflects the school's dedication to innovation and its proactive approach to incorporating cutting-edge technology and research into its curriculum. By focusing on these key areas, the institution aims to equip its students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

The core values of leadership, excellence, humility, and service form the bedrock of MRDA Academic Center's educational philosophy. These principles guide the institution's commitment to developing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also possess strong moral character and a desire to contribute positively to society. Through its holistic approach to education, MRDA Academic Center strives to mold future leaders who will make a lasting impact on their communities and beyond.
